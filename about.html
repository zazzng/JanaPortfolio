About

Jana is a UX/UI designer who believes that the best interfaces are the ones you never have to think about.

Currently studying design in Toronto, I work at the intersection of visual design and user experience — building interfaces that are not just beautiful, but deeply considered. My process starts with understanding people: their needs, their frustrations, and the moments where a product can make something genuinely easier.

I think in systems. Whether it's a component library or a navigation flow, I care about how the pieces fit together. A single screen is only as good as the logic that leads you to it — and the understanding of the person using it.

Outside of screen-based work, I draw a lot, read about typography, and spend too much time thinking about restaurant menus. I believe design is everywhere — and that paying close attention to the everyday world makes you a sharper, more empathetic designer.

I'm currently looking for internship and junior design opportunities where I can contribute meaningfully and keep growing fast.
